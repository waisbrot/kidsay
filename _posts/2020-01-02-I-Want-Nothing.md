Me: "Phineas, would you like some lunch?"<br/>
Phineas: "No."<br/>
Me: "Maybe some chicken? Or ham?"<br/>
Phineas: "No."<br/>
Me: "Or cheese?"<br/>
Phineas: "No. Whatever you say, I'll say 'no'."<br/>
Me: "Cookies?"<br/>
Phineas slowly turns and looks me directly in the eyes: "No."<br/>
