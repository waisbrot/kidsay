Phineas: You attacked me! I'll _sue_ you!<br/>
Ezra: I didn't _attack_ you. Did I _violently assault_ you?<br/>
Phineas: Well, you _in_sulted me.<br/>
Ezra: That's called _verbally_ assaulting.
